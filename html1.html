About

Jon, a Singapore-based potter, has dedicated the past two decades to exploring various art forms, including drawing, painting, photography, and jazz music.

However, it was pottery that resonated with Jon the most, allowing him to truly express himself. His passion lies in creating functional ceramics that elevate everyday rituals, transforming them into delightful experiences.

With a love for coffee and a growing interest in tea, Jon appreciates how the simplicity of forms can enhance the sensory perception of beverages, driving him to constantly seek innovative approaches.

Jon actively shares and documents his work on Instagram and sells his works here. For any additional inquiries, please feel free to send a direct message via Instagram DM or email.
